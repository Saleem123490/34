Ethical Dilemma ‚Äî Data Manipulation Request

Being situated as an IT Supervisor, one of the most probable ethical dilemmas is management pressuring an employee to edit performance data to make team productivity appear higher than it is.

Situation

Management requests data edits to inflate productivity. That creates a conflict between organizational expectations and professional honesty.

Decision & Rationale

I would refuse to falsify performance data and instead practice transparency. Integrity drives trust and long-term organizational health; manipulated data may help short-term optics but damages credibility, morale, and decision-making.

Reference: Hadar-Shoval, D. et al. (2024). Embedded values-like shape ethical reasoning of large language models on primary care ethical dilemmas. Heliyon.

Action Plan

  1. Explain accurate data and implications to management, inviting collaboration to improve outcomes.
  2. Document requests and decisions to preserve accountability.
  3. Implement training/metrics changes to track real improvement rather than masking performance.
